About Us
We are Hybrutos Racing, the official Formula Student team of SRMIST, Tamil Nadu,India. Our goal is to educate the next generation of engineers on building a sustainable FSAE car with the latest technologies and to spread awareness about it.We design,build and compete with our hybrid racing car in various competitions like Formula Hybrid,USA and Formula Imperial,India.
